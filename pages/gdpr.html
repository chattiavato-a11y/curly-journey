GDPR & Data Rights

Last updated:
Note: This page explains how to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data where applicable.

1) Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data.

  • Access: request a copy of personal data we hold.
  • Correction: request updates to inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: request deletion where permitted.
  • Restriction/Objection: request limits or object to certain processing.

2) What we may need to verify

To protect you, we may need to verify identity and confirm request scope. Do not send sensitive IDs in plain text.

3) How to submit a request

Use our Contact page and select “Privacy/GDPR” in your message, or describe your request clearly.

Submit a Request Privacy Policy

4) Response timeline

We aim to respond within a reasonable time based on the request type and legal requirements.

5) Where this applies

This page is informational and does not expand or limit any legal rights. Applicable rights depend on jurisdiction.
