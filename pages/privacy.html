Privacy Policy

Last updated:
Important: Please do not enter sensitive personal information in chat or forms (e.g., IDs, bank details, passwords).

1) What this policy covers

This policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website, use our contact forms, or interact with our chat experience.

2) Information we collect

We only collect information that is needed to provide and improve the service.

  • Contact details you submit (name, email, company, message).
  • Chat text you choose to provide. Avoid including personal sensitive details.
  • Basic technical data (e.g., browser type, device type, approximate location, and logs needed for security/abuse prevention).

3) How we use information

  • To respond to your requests and deliver services.
  • To secure the site and prevent abuse (rate limiting, fraud detection, audit logs).
  • To improve the chat experience and service quality. Where enabled, chat interactions may be used to improve or train the experience.
Privacy & Consent Terms

4) Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for business operations, security, and legal obligations. Retention periods may vary by data type.

5) Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help operate the website or support communications, under appropriate safeguards.

6) Your choices

  • Do not submit sensitive information in chat or forms.
  • If consent is required for chat, you can deny consent and disable chat.
  • You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion where applicable.

7) Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date shows when changes were made.
